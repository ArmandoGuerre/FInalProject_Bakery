Our Mission


Welcome to Guerrero's Bakery! Our mission here at Guerrero's Bakery is to happily serve our customers delcious mexican baked goods. We are a small family owned business that specializes in making hispanic baked treats, cakes, bread, cookies, and flan! Our family decided to open this establishment to support the hispanic community and bring a new diverse twist to the southside of Saskatchewan, Canada. We've recieved and evolved all the recipes from Grandma back in Mexico. Her pride and love for baking for her kids was inspiring and therefore, the Guerrero Family wanted to carry on that love here in Canada.




Ever since the Guerrero family migrated to Canada during the year 1971, we've been making baked goods for the community of Saskatchewan. We have an incredibly talended team that is continuously learning and growing to develop their baking skills and to ultimately satisfy our customers.We offer baked goods everyday and also have introduced catering so you can impressive your family, friends, or coworkers to a lovley breakfast party. You can find our links to our social media as well as a contact form on our "Contact Page" to get in touch with us and we could answer any questions you may have.
